Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and sea. It is often assosiated with depth and stability. It symbolizes trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligece, faith, truth and heaven.

Green

Green is the color of nature. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness and fertility. Green has strong emotional correspondance with safety.

Red

Red is the color of fire and blood, so it is assosiated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire and love.
